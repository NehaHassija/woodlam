Who We Are

Pune's Trusted Name in Premium Laminates

Woodlam – Interior Solutions is Pune's exclusive destination for Heritage, Hutch, and Kiverli laminates. We are passionate about helping homeowners, interior designers, and architects discover the perfect surface finish for every project — from residential kitchens and wardrobes to large commercial interiors.

As an exclusive authorized dealer for three of the industry's most respected laminate brands, we offer access to the complete range — including limited editions and new launches — before anyone else in the region.

Our showroom in Pune is designed to give you a hands-on experience — feel the textures, see the finishes in person, and get expert guidance from our team to make the right choice for your space.
