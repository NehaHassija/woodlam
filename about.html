Who We Are

Pune's Trusted Name in Premium Laminates

Woodlam – Interior Solutions is Pune's exclusive destination for Heritage, Hutch, and Kiverli laminates. We are passionate about helping homeowners, interior designers, and architects discover the perfect surface finish for every project.

As an exclusive authorized dealer for three of the industry's most respected laminate brands, we offer the complete range — including limited editions and new launches — before anyone else in the region.

Our showroom in Pune is designed to give you a hands-on experience. Feel the textures, see finishes in person, and get expert guidance to make the right choice for your space.
